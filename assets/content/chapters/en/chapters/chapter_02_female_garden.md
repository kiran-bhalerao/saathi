## The Three Doors

Your body has **three separate openings** down there. Not one, not two ‚Äî **three!**

Many women don't know this. Even some men don't!

- Located *above* the vaginal opening
- Nothing goes IN here ‚Äî only urine comes OUT

### Door 2: The Vaginal Opening (The Main Entrance)

### Door 3: The Anus (The Back Door)

## The Vulva vs. The Vagina

Here's something important:

**Vulva** = Everything you can see from the outside
- The lips (outer and inner)
- The clitoral hood
- The openings

**Vagina** = Only the inner tunnel
- You cannot see it from outside
- It's the passage that leads inside

Most people say "vagina" when they mean "vulva." Now you know the difference!

## The Lips (Labia)

You have two sets of "lips":

### Outer Lips (Labia Majora)

- Fatty, fleshy folds on the outside

- Protect everything inside

### Inner Lips (Labia Minora)

- Thinner, softer folds inside the outer lips
- Don't have hair
- Very sensitive to touch
- Can be different sizes ‚Äì some peek out, some don't

**Important:** Every woman's lips look different!

Some are long, some are short. Some are even, some are not. Some are darker, some are lighter.

**All of these are normal. There is no "correct" shape.**

## Is Down There "Dirty"?

Let's bust this myth right now:

**Your vulva is NOT dirty.**

In fact, the vagina is a self-cleaning organ! It produces natural discharge that keeps it healthy.

This discharge is normal:

- Changes throughout your monthly cycle
- Doesn't have a bad smell (a slight natural scent is fine)

**Hygiene tips:**
- Wash the *outside* (vulva) with plain water
- NEVER put soap *inside* the vagina ‚Äî it destroys healthy bacteria
- Wear breathable cotton underwear
- Change pads/panties regularly during periods

## New Words You'll Know

**Vulva**: The whole outside area you can see (lips, hood, openings).

**Labia**: The "lips" (outer and inner folds of skin).

**Clitoral Hood**: The small fold of skin covering the clitoris.
