## Let's Talk About the Myths

Before we go further, let's clear up some common misconceptions:

**Myth 2: "It's always painful"**

**Myth 4: "If you enjoy it, something is wrong with you"**

Reality: The anus has many nerve endings. Pleasure is a natural bodily response. There's nothing wrong with your body responding to touch.

**Myth 5: "It's unhygienic and disgusting"**

Reality: With proper preparation (covered below), anal play can be as clean as any other intimate activity. The disgust is often cultural conditioning, not biological fact.
